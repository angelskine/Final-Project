About Ayeppol (she/her)

She is a freelance illustrator with academic background in Environmental Studies. She has worked on freelance projects with bakeries, marketing agencies, local media firms, etc. She likes to draw the little things but is also unafraid to take on visual projects for big social movements. Outside of her normal routine, you will probably find her travelling in the most random cities looking for the best yoghurt.

With experience in food and beverage, marketing, and a bit of coding, she thrives on translating complex stories into attractive visual-storytelling.
